FAQ

How do I book a service on the home service platform?

To book a service, simply log in to your account, browse the available service categories, select the desired service, choose a preferred date and time, and complete the booking process. You'll receive a confirmation once the booking is confirmed.

Can I schedule recurring maintenance tasks through the platform?

Yes, you can schedule recurring maintenance tasks by selecting the option for recurring appointments during the booking process. You can specify the frequency and duration of the recurring appointments according to your needs.

How are service providers vetted for reliability and quality?

Service providers undergo a rigorous vetting process, including background checks, verification of licenses and certifications, and review of customer feedback and ratings. Only qualified and reputable service providers are approved to offer services on our platform.

How are pricing and payment handled for the services?

Pricing for services is determined by the service providers and displayed on their service listings. Payment is typically processed securely through the platform, and you may have the option to pay online or in-person directly to the service provider upon completion of the service.

Can I communicate directly with service providers before booking?

Yes, you can communicate directly with service providers through the platform's Contact Number. This allows you to discuss specific requirements, ask questions, and clarify any details before finalizing your booking.

Are there any additional fees or hidden charges for using the platform?

We strive to be transparent about pricing, and there are typically no additional fees or hidden charges for using the platform. The price you see on the service listings is the price you'll pay, unless otherwise specified by the service provider.
